1. Pick up tile "o" from floor
2. Place tile "o" on wall to spell "boo"
3. Pick up key from table
4. Click fireplace to get passcode to exit door
5. Click on recliner to find lockbox, use key from table to unlock
6. Pick up feather duster from recliner
7. Dust cobwebs from light above recliner and get key to exit door
8. Click on coffin to find exit door
9. Use second key and passcode to escape
